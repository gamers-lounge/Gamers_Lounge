Our Menu

FOOD AND DRINK AT THE
BAD MOON CAFE

Playing tabletop games has never tasted better. Bad Moon Cafe offers a large selection of delicious food, as well as a range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. We have wood-fired pizza, freshly baked pastries, artisan sandwiches, barista coffee, craft beer, a fully-stocked cocktail bar, and much more. Gluten free and vegan options subject to availability.​

PASTRIES, SANDWICHES & snacks

We have fresh pastries baked daily in the cafe.  We offer a selection of croissants, pain au chocolat, and brownies.  We also have freshly prepared artisan sandwiches, with a wide selection of meat and meat-free options.  Gluten free and vegan options often available.  Last but not least, we have a large array of yummy cookies, crisps, and confectionery for that all-important energy boost while you play.

Pizzas

We have teamed up with Basilico to bring you traditional Italian pizza while you play!  Each pizza is hand thrown and baked to order in wood-fired ovens using top quality Italian ingredients.  Gluten free bases available.  Pizzas come in two sizes – 10 inch and 18 inch.  We recommend the 10 inch for one and the 18 inch for 2-3 people or one very hungry person.
